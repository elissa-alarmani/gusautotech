Our Services

Automotive Diagnostics

We provide comprehensive mechanical and electrical diagnostics for all makes and models. Whether your check engine light is on or your car is acting up, we scan your vehicle's systems, pinpoint the exact problem, and explain it to you in plain language. We read fault codes, diagnose the root cause, and clear codes once the issue is resolved. All at your location.

  • Mechanical diagnostics
  • Electrical diagnostics
  • Check engine light diagnosis & code clearing
